About Me

Born and raised in Denver, Colorado since 1991 (though this pic is from California)

Grew up surrounded by the music my father would play: alternative rock music, grunge, metal, and classic rock

Bands that include Pearl Jam, Smashing Pumpkins, The Offspring, Nirvana, Soundgarden, Metallica, System of a Down, Tool, Pantera, AC/DC, Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd

One of the first things I could say as a toddler was "Pearl Jam"

This music has had a major impact in my life from childhood to today

Lots of my music has flavors from these styles, often subconsciously

At a young age, my mother would play soft jazz on the radio for me to fall asleep

I would imagine different melodies in my mind, something that has developed greatly over time

Received my first guitar at age 12 from my parents at Christmas, consistanly pressured by my father to learn

A very slow and frustrating start didn't get me very far

It wasn't until age 16 that I actually started to be able to play

Thanks to the video game Guitar Hero, it gave me the inspiration and desire to try again

After beating every song in that game on "expert," I knew it was time to take things to the next level, do it on the real thing

When I discovered guitar tabs, I finally found a system that allowed me to learn the songs I loved

The first song I attempted to play was Iron Man by Black Sabbath

For the next few years I played with my brother, who was a drummer in school, and he learned to play the drum kit

Since then I have join a few different bands, trying to find my place in the musical world

I'm still trying to find my place to this day, experimenting with different genres, sounds, recording techniques, music making methods...

I have taken college courses in music theory, physics of sound, and computer science

Practiced music composition using Guitar Pro, Fruity Loops Studio, Ableton Live, and Audiacity

Learned a bit about mixing and mastering from professionals online

Now I'm learning how to incorporate God into my music and praise him with song
